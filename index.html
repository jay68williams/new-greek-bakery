Our story

From the grill to the oven

New Greek started on Chillingham Road in Heaton, serving authentic Greek street food. The gyros, the skewers, the homemade dishes. Newcastle took notice.

Now we are expanding into something new. A dedicated Greek bakery cafe in the heart of Jesmond. Same commitment to doing things properly. Same family. A completely different experience.

Fresh pastries at dawn. Greek coffee all day. A place to sit, slow down and eat something real.
